About INKPOOL

Inkpool is a writing competition organised by Blogscord incorporation and powered by Schaffen Softwares Pvt. Ltd. The event takes place from 21st to 26th September, widely in 3 rounds and will be supervised by Blogscord's and Schaffen softwares' dedicated team. With inkpool our aim is to bring out the writer every person hides in themself and polish the skills of budding writers by offering them to an online platform. To unbox the inherent writer in them so that they could make a difference.

Where?

The whole event will be held online on Blogscord

When?

21st-26th September, 2020

FAQs

What's the cost of the event?

The event cost is a minimal of Rs.30! We'll also provide you with enough swags, goodies and discount coupons for various platform

What's the application process like?

You're required to register with the link given and then make a blogscord account, using wix. In this way you'll be able to publish your writings for the competition on our website. The exact details will be conveyed post registration

Where will the event take place?

The whole event will be conducted online on Blogscord itself.

Do I need to have any pre-requisites for the competition?

If you love to write, you're more than welcome to participate in the competition

What's in it for me?

There are exciting prizes for top 3 winners of the competition and those of fun activities, discount coupons for popular restaurants, and vouchers and subscriptions for various vouchers including souled store and expertrons
