Inkpool is a writing competition organised by Blogscord incorporation and powered by Schaffen Softwares Pvt. Ltd.
The event takes place from 1st to 7th September, widely in 3 rounds and will be supervised by Blogscord's and Schaffen softwares' 
dedicated team. With inkpool our aim is to bring out the writer  every person hides in themself and polish the skills of budding 
writers by offering them to an online platform. To unbox the inherent writer in them so that they could make a difference.
